ABOUT US

    What makes our coffee special?


      We wanted to share a little about our coffees, and why we think they are so special.
      First we set the grinder, depending on how coarse we want the coffee to be.
      Then we check the water temperature — the lower the temperature, the less coffee extraction.
      We don’t want coffee that’s too strong because that would give it a bitter taste,
      but also not one that is too weak because then its flavor won’t be distinct enough.
